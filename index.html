About AgroAI

Project Overview

AgroAI is a real-time plant leaf disease detection system built on the YOLOv8 object detection architecture. It classifies 10 distinct disease conditions from a single leaf photograph and delivers immediate severity ratings, evidence-based treatment protocols, and actionable prevention recommendations — enabling faster, data-driven decisions for farmers and agronomists.
